# Programme Planner

The Programme Planner should match how camps are actually planned.

Camps are usually planned in blocks:

- Friday Evening
- Saturday Morning Activities
- Saturday Afternoon Rotation
- Saturday Campfire
- Sunday Pack Down

## Core Concepts

### Programme Block

A meaningful section of the programme.

### Activity

A definition of something that can happen.

### Programme Session

A scheduled occurrence of an activity.

### Rotation Planner

Generates group rotations from groups, activities and time slots.
