Planned Import Behaviour

OSM member exports are normally downloaded by section, not one person at a time. The future importer should allow a section export to be uploaded, then match one row to this person.

The importer should update only the fields we actually need for camp:

  • phone and email contact details
  • primary contact details
  • emergency contact details
  • allergies and allergy action notes
  • medication
  • medical notes
  • dietary requirements

The importer should not import unnecessary data by default, such as home address, school or Gift Aid fields.

Upload and matching are not active yet. This is the entry point for the next importer stage.
